# EMURGO Corporate Chronology Conflict
**Date:** 2026-04-08
**Question:** Can EMURGO's own public corporate timeline help resolve whether the `781,381,495 ADA` sale-sized genesis entry could have been originally purchased by EMURGO itself?

## Official EMURGO Sources Conflict

I found three different chronology narratives on official `emurgo.io` pages.

### Timeline A — Founded in 2017 / registered in Japan

Early official EMURGO pages describe the company simply as founded in 2017.

Example:

- `EMURGO Announces ADA Token will be launched on OKEx` (2018)  
  https://www.emurgo.io/press-news/okex-listed/

### Timeline B — Tokyo since June 2017

Multiple 2019 EMURGO press pages include an `About EMURGO` section stating that EMURGO was:

- registered in Tokyo, Japan since June 2017
- registered in Singapore since May 2018

Examples:

- `EMURGO Joins Public Chain Technology Alliance As New Member` (2019-04-18)  
  https://www.emurgo.io/press-news/emurgo-joins-public-chain-technology-alliance-as-new-member/
- `Cardano’s ADA and Yoroi Wallet Now Fully Integrated with the Ledger Nano S` (2019-03-29)  
  https://www.emurgo.io/press-news/cardanos-ada-and-yoroi-wallet-now-fully-integrated-with-the-ledger-nano-s/
- `EMURGO and Docomo Open Innovation Cloud to Launch a Digital Payment Platform in the MENA Region` (2019-08-30)  
  https://www.emurgo.io/press-news/emurgo-and-docomo-open-innovation-cloud-launch-digital-payment-platform-mena-region/

### Timeline C — Established in 2015 in Japan

Multiple 2023-2024 EMURGO press pages instead describe EMURGO as:

- established in 2015 in Japan

Examples:

- `EMURGO Partners with Huawei Cloud to Expand the Cardano Network` (2024-04-11)  
  https://www.emurgo.io/press-news/emurgo-partners-with-huawei-cloud-to-expand-the-cardano-network/
- `EMURGO Engages With GSR to Strengthen Cardano Blockchain Ecosystem` (2024-05-21)  
  https://www.emurgo.io/press-news/emurgo-engages-with-gsr-to-strengthen-cardano-blockchain-ecosystem/

### Timeline D — Current About Page Avoids Any Year

The current official `About` page does not resolve the conflict. It presents EMURGO as a co-founding Cardano entity and lists current offices, but it does **not** state `2015` or `June 2017`.

Example:

- `About | Helping People And Organizations Unlock Web3`  
  https://www.emurgo.io/about/

## Why This Matters

The Cardano voucher sale ran from September 2015 through January 2017.

If the `June 2017` timeline is the relevant legal start date, then the `781,381,495 ADA` sale-sized entry is less likely to be a straightforward "EMURGO company bought it during the sale" case, and more likely to involve:

- a predecessor vehicle,
- an affiliated company,
- a founder-linked company later folded into EMURGO, or
- a later custody transfer into EMURGO operations.

If the `2015` timeline is materially accurate, then EMURGO or an EMURGO precursor remains chronologically compatible with participation during the sale era.

## Best Current Use Of This Evidence

This chronology evidence is not yet strong enough to resolve attribution.

What it **does** support is a narrower claim:

> EMURGO's own official public materials are internally inconsistent on when EMURGO began, so corporate start date arguments should currently be treated as unresolved rather than decisive.

The pattern is now:

- early official press boilerplate: `founded in 2017`
- later official press boilerplate: `June 2017`
- newest official press boilerplate: `2015`
- current company page: no year at all

That looks more like a moving official narrative than a single stable company chronology.
